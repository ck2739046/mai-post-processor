ref: https://github.com/upscayl/upscayl v2.15.0

编辑run.bat指令:
   [--size]               required, size of final output image, int(200, 5000)
   [--upscayl]         required, toggle upscayl, 0 disable 1 enable 
   [--model]           optional, model upscayl used, default "digital-art-4x"
   [--scale]             optional, resolution scale, int(1, 8), default "1" 

对于1080p, --scale推荐选1，对于4k屏，推荐选2
